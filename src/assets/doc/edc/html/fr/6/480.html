Les types d'articles dans le contenu

By default, in edc, we differentiate six article types:

  • About: general descriptive information
  • Prerequisites: steps required before using an option or feature. These are most often used in UI bricks
  • Usage: steps for carrying out a task, using a UI brick
  • Example: results or steps. Can be avoided in contextualised help
  • How to: not usage, extra tasks users want to carry out
  • Pitfall: problems a user could encounter.

You can create new article types as needed in Configuration > Content management > Topologies. If necessary, you can also give your article types an alias.
